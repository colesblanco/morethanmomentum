Privacy Policy

More Than Momentum LLC  ·  Last updated: April 18, 2026

Overview

More Than Momentum ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website located at morethanmomentum.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website or contact us about our services.

We keep this simple and honest — consistent with how we run our business. We do not sell your personal information. We do not share it with advertisers. We collect only what we need to respond to your inquiries and deliver our services.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you contact us through our website's contact form, including:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your business name (optional)
  • The service you're interested in (optional)
  • Any message or details you choose to include

We do not collect payment information, social security numbers, or any sensitive personal data through this website. We do not require account creation or login to browse our site.

How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through our contact form is used solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry or service request
  • Provide information about our services that is relevant to your question
  • Follow up on a proposal or ongoing engagement

We do not use your contact information to add you to marketing lists without your explicit consent, and we will never sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services that may process data as part of their normal operation:

  • Formspree — Our contact form submissions are processed by Formspree (formspree.io). Formspree receives and forwards your form data to our email. You can review Formspree's privacy policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.
  • Cloudflare — Our website is hosted on Cloudflare Pages and served through Cloudflare's global network. Cloudflare may process request metadata (such as IP addresses) as part of its security and performance services. See cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.
  • Google Fonts — We load web fonts from Google's servers, which may result in Google receiving your IP address as part of the request. See policies.google.com/privacy.
  • GoHighLevel — If you become a client, your contact information may be stored in GoHighLevel, our CRM platform. GoHighLevel is used internally to manage client relationships and follow-up communications. See gohighlevel.com/privacy-policy.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website does not use first-party tracking cookies or advertising pixels. We do not use Facebook Pixel, Google Ads remarketing tags, or similar ad-network tracking on this site.

Third-party services listed above (Cloudflare, Google Fonts) may set technical cookies or log request data as part of their infrastructure operations. These are not used for advertising or behavioral profiling by us.

If we add analytics tools in the future, this policy will be updated prior to their use.

Data Retention

Contact form submissions forwarded to our email are retained for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and for reasonable business record-keeping purposes. If you would like your information removed from our records, please contact us directly and we will honor the request promptly.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information from our records
  • Withdraw consent for communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us directly at the email address below. We will respond within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Our website and services are intended for business owners and professionals. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 13. If you believe a minor has submitted information through our site, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as our services evolve or legal requirements change. When we do, we will update the "last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
